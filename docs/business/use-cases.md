## Primary Use Case: Education Attendance

### University Lecture Attendance

**Scenario:**
- Professor teaching CS101 to 200 students
- 50-minute lecture in large auditorium
- Traditional attendance takes 10-15 minutes

**With Temporal 3D QR:**
1. Professor opens web app on laptop
2. Projects animated QR on screen (auto-starts)
3. Students open scanner app (one-time install)
4. Point phone at screen (auto-captures 200ms)
5. Attendance marked instantly
6. **Total time: 2-3 minutes for entire class**

**Benefits:**
- ✅ 80% time saving (2 min vs 10-15 min)
- ✅ No proxy attendance (screenshot-proof)
- ✅ Works even if WiFi is down (offline mode)
- ✅ Audit trail (timestamped records)
- ✅ Real-time dashboard for professor

**Market Size:**
- India: 45,000+ universities
- Students per university: 5,000-50,000
- Lectures per day: 10-50
- **Addressable market:** ₹2,250 crore/year
